Ruby

Ruby is a dynamic, object-oriented programming language known for its simplicity and elegance. Created by Yukihiro Matsumoto, it emphasizes productivity and ease of use with a focus on natural language-like syntax. Ruby's most famous framework, Ruby on Rails, revolutionized web development by providing a robust structure for building complex applications quickly. Its vibrant community and extensive libraries further enhance its appeal, making it a popular choice for both beginners and seasoned developers.
